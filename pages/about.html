Meet Diogo

If our first meeting was through my instagram then surely you've already seen my face, my weird laugh and a bit of my personality so... welcome, I'm very proud to have you here. if you want to see some pictures that are different from the usual, go to my pages on instagram and take a look at my work

@safanelliphotography @zooninmoments @safanelliphotographer

I'm Diogo, 34 years old, the face hiding behind the cameras that will get you excited during our work together (and very likely even more excited throughout your instagram posts) I came from Curitiba Brazil, to Ireland to explore my photographic side. With over 7 years of experience in landscape, portrait and lifestyle photography, I founded Safanelli Photography. I will be happy to bring out the photogenic side of everyone with a laid-back side.

You were there a little discouraged and thinking that you wouldn't do anything very important today, go to your wardrobe, put on a cool outfit, come to Dublin because I'm here waiting for you to change the mood of this day and make history.

I'm in love with my Marketer and Advertiser (she's the one who makes all this happen, believe me), dad of Aurnia, a beautiful Irish Golden retriever lady from Cork, crazy enough to pack my bags go anywhere and record unforgettable moments.

That's just a little bit about Diogo Safanelli, your favorite photographer from now on!
